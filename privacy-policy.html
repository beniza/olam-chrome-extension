Privacy Policy for Olam Dictionary Extension

Last Updated: November 6, 2025

Overview

Olam Dictionary Extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension provides English-Malayalam dictionary lookups and does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information Collection and Use

The extension does NOT collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your browsing history
  • We do not collect personal identification information
  • We do not track your searches or usage patterns
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Data Storage

The extension stores the following data locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API:

  • Search History: Your recent searches are stored locally to provide quick access. This data never leaves your device.
  • User Preferences: Settings like your preferred search mode (exact/prefix/suffix/substring) are stored locally.

All stored data remains on your device and can be cleared at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the extension, or
  • Clearing your browser's extension data

External API Usage

The extension uses the Olam.in Dictionary API (https://olam.in) to retrieve dictionary definitions. When you perform a search:

  • Only your search query is sent to the Olam.in API
  • No personal information is transmitted
  • The API is provided by the SMC (Swathanthra Malayalam Computing) project

We recommend reviewing Olam.in's privacy policy for information about how they handle search queries: https://olam.in

Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • contextMenus: To add the "Search in Olam Dictionary" option when you right-click on selected text
  • scripting: To enable the context menu functionality on web pages
  • storage: To save your search history and preferences locally on your device
  • host_permissions (https://olam.in/*): To communicate with the Olam.in Dictionary API for retrieving definitions

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality and nothing else.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used by this extension is the Olam.in Dictionary API. We do not integrate any advertising networks, analytics services, or other third-party trackers.

Children's Privacy

This extension does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The extension is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You can:

  • Clear locally stored data by uninstalling the extension
  • Disable the extension at any time through Chrome's extension settings

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the extension, please contact us:

  • GitHub: https://github.com/beniza/olam-chrome-extension
